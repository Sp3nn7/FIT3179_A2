How many fatalities do these attacks cause

As can be seen from figure 7, this boxplot describes the distribution of fatalities from terrorist attacks from 2014-2017 for specific weapon types. Throughout this time period, although there have been countless terrorist attacks (refer to figures 1-5) the fatalities are actually quite low, where the median amount of fatalities for each of these weapons is 1 fatality.
